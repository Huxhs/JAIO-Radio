Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jaio Radio (“we,” “us”) at jaioradio.com collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or use our forms.

Information we collect

We may collect the following when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact form: name, email address, and message content.
  • Email drop list: email address and related signup metadata handled by our email service provider.
  • Technical data: basic analytics or server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) if enabled by our hosting provider.

How we use your information

  • Respond to booking, collaboration, or general inquiries.
  • Send updates about new music, drops, and news if you subscribed to the drop list.
  • Improve the website and protect against abuse or spam.

Third-party services

We use trusted third parties to operate parts of this site. Data you submit may be processed by:

  • Formspree — contact form delivery (Formspree Privacy Policy).
  • Mailchimp (or similar) — email list management (Intuit / Mailchimp Privacy).
  • Spotify — embedded music players (Spotify Privacy Policy).

These providers have their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

Email communications

If you join the drop list, you may receive promotional emails from Jaio Radio. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any marketing email or by contacting us.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To make a request, use the contact form on our site.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us via the contact section at jaioradio.com.
